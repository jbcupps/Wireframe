About this Visualization

This interactive tool visualizes Spacetime Klein Bottles (SKBs) representing quark structures in a topological model:

  • Individual Mode: Shows three sub-SKBs representing two up quarks (red and green) and one down quark (blue).
  • Merged Mode: Shows a stable SKB representing a baryon (like a proton or neutron).
  • Twist Parameters: Control the topological properties of each quark.

You can rotate the 3D visualization by clicking and dragging, and zoom with the scroll wheel.
