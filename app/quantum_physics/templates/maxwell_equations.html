  • The electromagnetic field tensor \(F_{\mu\nu}\) corresponds to the curvature of a U(1) gauge connection in 4D manifold space.

  • The SKB (Spin-Klein-Bottle) structures in 4D manifest as electromagnetic fields in our 3D projection.
