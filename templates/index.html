About this Visualization

This interactive tool visualizes Spacetime Klein Bottles (SKBs) as a topological model for fundamental particles:

  • Individual Mode: Shows three sub-SKBs with distinct colors:
    • ■ Sub-SKB 1 (Orange-red)
    • ■ Sub-SKB 2 (Blue)
    • ■ Sub-SKB 3 (Green)
  • Merged Mode: Shows a stable SKB representing a composite particle.
  • Multi-Dimensional Twists: Control the topological properties along X, Y, and Z axes for each sub-SKB. Higher twist values create more complex structures.
  • Loop Factor: Adjusts the number of topological loops in the structure, related to binding energy.
  • Time Parameter: Simulates evolution along the time dimension, representing closed timelike curves (CTCs).

You can rotate the 3D visualization by clicking and dragging, and zoom with the scroll wheel. The thicker lines and distinct colors make it easier to distinguish between different sub-SKBs and observe their twists and loops.
