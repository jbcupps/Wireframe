Physics & Mathematics Notes

  • Holonomy product: The product of the three color holonomies around the triple junction must be +1 for a globally consistent Pin⁻ structure on the composite.
  • Bordism class: The class \(k \mod 16\) in \(\Omega_4^{\text{Pin}^-}\) measures the topological “oddness” of the junction. Non-zero classes are still allowed because the 4-manifold bounds in a higher-dimensional embedding or in a multi-baryon system.
  • Causal Compensation Principle (CCP): When the three SKBs approach, the internal CTCs must be compensated by the surrounding geometry. Strong-force tentacles visualize the “knotting” that enforces this compensation — separation costs infinite energy (confinement).
  • Maxwell flux tubes (EM mode): The thin pulsing tubes between defects carry quantized flux \(\frac{1}{2\pi}\oint F = Q\), reproducing the Coulomb field at long range.
