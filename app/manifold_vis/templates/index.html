What are Spacetime Klein Bottles?

Spacetime Klein Bottles (SKBs) are four-dimensional non-orientable manifolds that can be used to model fundamental particles in physics. Key properties include:

Topological Properties
  • Non-orientability: A global property where a system cannot be consistently oriented
  • Closed timelike curves: Paths that loop back in time
  • Genus and Euler characteristic: Fundamental invariants of the topology
  • Linking numbers and braiding: Relationships between multiple manifolds
